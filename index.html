Course Description

This course introduces the concepts of air pollution, sources of pollutants, meteorological aspects affecting pollutant dispersion, air quality monitoring techniques, and pollution control strategies used to improve environmental air quality.

Course Outcomes

  • CO1 – Explain the sources of air pollution and different types of pollutants
  • CO2 – Describe the effects of air pollutants on health, vegetation, animals and materials
  • CO3 – Discuss methods of ambient air quality monitoring
  • CO4 – Explain meteorological aspects affecting dispersion of pollutants
  • CO5 – Describe air pollution control technologies
